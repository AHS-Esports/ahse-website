For those who do not wish to compete, we also offer training and experience in the esports industry such as broadcast production, shoutcasting, reporting, and more.

How can I join AHS Esports?

AHS Esports is open during the year at all times. Feel free to join us during club rush or stop by any time at Mr. Elam's room to get more information. For better resources visit our discord and dm any of the officers for more information!

What Games Does AHS Esports Play?

AHS Esports plays a vareity of games. Many of our games like Valorant, Rainbow Six Siege, Minecraft, and Chess are standard and are played at all times in the year. Other games are also played throughout the year at different stages.

How do I become an Officer?

The AHS Esports club has various officer position opening at different times throughout the year. We offer lower-level positions during the fall where officers can get an understanding of how the club runs and functions. Towards, the end of the year, executive positions and other high level officer positions are offered for the next school year.
